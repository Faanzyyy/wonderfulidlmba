Gunung Bromo

Mount Bromo is located in Bromo Tengger Semeru National Park, East Java, on the border of Probolinggo, Malang, Pasuruan and Lumajang Regencies.



Description

Mount Bromo is a major tourist attraction in East Java. The stunning beauty of the landscape, including views of the sunrise and millions of stars at night, is a major attraction. Some tourist spots that must be visited on Mount Bromo are: View Point Penanjakan 1 and Penanjakan 2, Mount Bromo Crater. The mountain has a height of 2393 m above sea level with cold weather which is characteristic of Mount Bromo with natural mountain views which are truly enchanting for anyone who comes to visit it, supported by several natural tourist attractions in the surrounding area making this mountain a popular tourist spot. unique and fun.
