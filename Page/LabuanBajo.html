Labuan Bajo

Labuan Bajo is located at the western tip of the large island of Flores in West Manggarai Regency, Nusa Tenggara region in eastern Indonesia.



Description

Labuan Bajo is the capital of West Manggarai Regency which has a very strategic geographical location, where Labuan Bajo is located in the western part of the island of Flores. Labuan Bajo is also known as a tourism city which is the western gateway to enter the tourist charm of Flores Island. The beauty of Komodo Island with its rich local culture and exotic islands around it makes Labuan Bajo a potential and frequently visited tourist destination.
