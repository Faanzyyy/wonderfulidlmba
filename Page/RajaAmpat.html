Raja Ampat

The Raja Ampat Islands are an archipelago located in the western part of the Bird's Head Peninsula (Vogelkoop) of Papua Island.



Description

The Raja Ampat Islands are a place that has great potential to be used as a tourist attraction, especially diving tourism. According to various sources, the waters of the Raja Ampat Islands are one of the 10 best waters for diving sites throughout the world. Clusters of coral are the main characteristic of the surface of Raja Ampat. The scenery that we will encounter when we explore Raja Ampat. Raja Ampat is a hidden paradise in eastern Indonesia, a group of beautiful islands called Piaynemo. The waters of Raja Ampat are relatively calm and friendly.
